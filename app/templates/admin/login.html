How to Sign (OpenSSH)

  1. Copy the challenge above.
  2. Save it to a file (no newline):

printf "%s" "{{ challenge }}" > challenge.txt

Sign it with your SSH private key:

ssh-keygen -Y sign -f ~/.ssh/id_ed25519 -n {{ ssh_namespace }} challenge.txt

This produces challenge.txt.sig. Print it:

cat challenge.txt.sig
